In a world of feature bloat, the most courageous act a Product Manager can take is to say "No."

### The Power of Subtraction

Every feature you add increases the cognitive surface area of your product. It gives the user one more thing to learn, one more thing to configure, and one more thing to break.

True minimalism isn't about white space or Helvetica fonts; it's about **clarity of purpose**. 

When we designed the new wallet architecture, we cut 40% of the existing features. The result? Engagement went up by 60%. By removing the noise, we allowed the signal—the core utility—to shine through.
