### Logic as an Art Form

Just as the Swiss Style of design relies on grids, mathematics, and strict rules to create beauty, software engineering relies on logic patterns and architectural purity. When we build products, we aren't just assembling bricks; we are composing a symphony of logic gates.

> "Clean code reads like well-written prose." - Robert C. Martin

In my work, whether optimizing a neural network or refining a UI interaction, I strive for that moment where complexity collapses into simplicity. That is the art of code.
